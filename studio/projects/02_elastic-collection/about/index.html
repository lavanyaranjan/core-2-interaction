what do i eat?

How many times a day do you wonder what to eat? Or more importantly, what to cook?

Balancing classes, extracurriculars, internships, social life, cleaning, and cooking can be difficult.

We're here to help you out!

'College Kid Recipes' is a collection of different one-hero-ingredient recipes that can be made easily by college students who need to adapt recipes to balance the ease of cooking and palette.

On this website we're trying to answer your question, one step at a time. Answer the first question, what are you thinking? And then get directed to a collection of recipes that can be sorted by your other requirements.

Remember, not everyone needs to be a masterchef, sometimes you just need sustenance and survival.
